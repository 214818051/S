Who are we?

Welcome to Samnko Books (Pty) Ltd, your premier self-publishing company based in South Africa!Established almost a decade ago in 2014, Samnko Books started as a sole trader, operating as a mobile bookstore right from the comfort of our sitting room. Over the years, fueled by our passion for words and their profound meaning, we've evolved into a multi-capable independent publishing powerhouse. In 2018, we proudly registered with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC), marking a significant milestone in our journey.At Samnko Books, we're not just a publishing company – we're a community of passionate individuals dedicated to bringing stories to life. From non-fiction to fiction and children's books, we specialize in a diverse range of genres, always striving to provide captivating reads for every age group.One of our core missions is to uplift and amplify the voices of emerging young black authors across Africa. Since our inception, we've collaborated with the Gauteng Department of Education, supplying over 2000 titles to school libraries throughout the province. Additionally, we've proudly published more than 400 books, fostering a supportive environment for writers to share their stories with the world.In 2021, we established the Samnko Community Foundation, a Non-Profit Company committed to capacity building, workshops, and seminars in creative and publishing fields. Through this initiative, we aim to nurture the next generation of storytellers and foster a love for literature within our community.Embracing the digital age, we offer authors the opportunity to see their words in print through digital distribution. Utilizing online platforms such as Amazon, Takealot.com, Google Books, Barnes & Noble, Kindle Direct Publishing, and more, we ensure that our publications reach a global audience.We understand the challenges aspiring authors face, which is why we offer personalized publishing consultations to guide you through the process and ensure your book reaches its target audience. With a project timeline ranging from 35 to 90 days, we're committed to delivering your book within the specified timeframe, thanks to our collaboration with experienced freelancers.At Samnko Books, we believe that every story deserves to be told, and we're here to make that journey a reality. Join us as we embark on a literary adventure together!

WAt Samnko Books, we are dedicated to providing a range of services centered around publishing, distribution, and book sales. Here's what we do:1. Self-Publishing Services: We offer comprehensive self-publishing services for authors who want to see their works in print. From manuscript editing and formatting to cover design and printing, we assist authors at every step of the publishing process.2. Book Distribution: We specialize in distributing books to various outlets, including schools, libraries, bookstores, and online retailers. Through our partnerships and networks, we ensure that our published titles reach a wide audience across South Africa and beyond.3. Educational Materials: In collaboration with the Gauteng Department of Education, we provide educational materials for school libraries, supporting learning initiatives and promoting literacy among students.4. Support for Emerging Authors: We actively seek to uplift emerging young black authors by providing a platform for them to showcase their talent and share their stories. Through our publishing services and promotional efforts, we help authors navigate the publishing industry and reach their target audience.5. Stationery Supply: In addition to books, we also supply stationery items to meet the needs of schools, students, and educational institutions.6. Samnko Education Foundation: Our non-profit arm, the Samnko Education Foundation, focuses on capacity building, workshops, and seminars in the creative and publishing fields. Through this initiative, we aim to empower aspiring writers and foster a culture of creativity and literacy.7. Digital Distribution: Embracing technology, we offer digital distribution services to make our titles accessible to a global audience through online platforms such as Amazon, Takealot.com, Google Books, Barnes & Noble, and Kindle Direct Publishing.8. Publishing Consultations: We provide personalized publishing consultations to guide authors through the publishing process, ensuring that their books meet industry standards and reach their intended audience.9. Timely Delivery: With a project timeline ranging from 35 to 90 days, we are committed to delivering high-quality books within the specified timeframe, thanks to our collaboration with experienced freelancers.At Samnko Books, we are passionate about words and storytelling. Quality Publishing

Young African emerging writers of various ages and areas are a major focus of ours. This is not to say that other age groups will not be considered; we are all open to a variety of submissions. With the advancement of technology, we offer you the opportunity to see your words in print through digital distribution, which is done through online distribution whereby your book can be accessed online at major stores such as Amazon, Takealot.com, Google Books, Barnes & Nobles, Kindle Direct Publishing, and much more other available. Our project timeline ranges from 35 to 90 days, allowing you to receive your book within the specified time frame after approval. Because of the nature of our team-building strategy, this is possible. It's heartbreaking to see your work rejected by a potential publisher after years or days of hard work and sleepless nights when all you wanted was to see your words in print and reap the benefits, spread the message, and add value to the book's development. There is no chance of your work being rejected by us, which is why we offer you a publishing consultation in which you will be given a fair chance to be given guidance on making sure your book reaches its target. It is our responsibility as writers to make you happy. You do not need to be concerned about any delays because we are collaborating with experienced freelancers.
