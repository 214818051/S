Who are we?

Welcome to Samnko Books (Pty) Ltd, your premier self-publishing company based in South Africa!Established almost a decade ago in 2014, Samnko Books started as a sole trader, operating as a mobile bookstore right from the comfort of our sitting room. Over the years, fueled by our passion for words and their profound meaning, we've evolved into a multi-capable independent publishing powerhouse. In 2018, we proudly registered with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC), marking a significant milestone in our journey.At Samnko Books, we're not just a publishing company â€“ we're a community of passionate individuals dedicated to bringing stories to life. From non-fiction to fiction and children's books, we specialize in a diverse range of genres, always striving to provide captivating reads for every age group. One of our core missions is to uplift and amplify the voices of emerging young black authors across Africa. Since our inception, we've collaborated with the Gauteng Department of Education, supplying over 2000 titles to school libraries throughout the province. Additionally, we've proudly published more than 400 books, fostering a supportive environment for writers to share their stories with the world.In 2021, we established the Samnko Community Foundation, a Non-Profit Company committed to capacity building, workshops, and seminars in creative and publishing fields. Through this initiative, we aim to nurture the next generation of storytellers and foster a love for literature within our community.Embracing the digital age, we offer authors the opportunity to see their words in print through digital distribution. Utilizing online platforms such as Amazon, Takealot.com, Google Books, Barnes & Noble, Kindle Direct Publishing, and more, we ensure that our publications reach a global audience.We understand the challenges aspiring authors face, which is why we offer personalized publishing consultations to guide you through the process and ensure your book reaches its target audience. With a project timeline ranging from 35 to 90 days, we're committed to delivering your book within the specified timeframe, thanks to our collaboration with experienced freelancers.At Samnko Books, we believe that every story deserves to be told, and we're here to make that journey a reality. Join us as we embark on a literary adventure together!

What we do?

h4>

Samnko Books, we are dedicated to providing a range of services centered around publishing, distribution, and book sales. Here's what we do:1. Self-Publishing Services: We offer comprehensive self-publishing services for authors who want to see their works in print. From manuscript editing and formatting to cover design and printing, we assist authors at every step of the publishing process.2. Book Distribution: We specialize in distributing books to various outlets, including schools, libraries, bookstores, and online retailers. Through our partnerships and networks, we ensure that our published titles reach a wide audience across South Africa and beyond.3. Educational Materials: In collaboration with the Gauteng Department of Education, we provide educational materials for school libraries, supporting learning initiatives and promoting literacy among students.4. Support for Emerging Authors: We actively seek to uplift emerging young black authors by providing a platform for them to showcase their talent and share their stories. Through our publishing services and promotional efforts, we help authors navigate the publishing industry and reach their target audience.5. Stationery Supply: In addition to books, we also supply stationery items to meet the needs of schools, students, and educational institutions.6. Samnko Education Foundation: Our non-profit arm, the Samnko Education Foundation, focuses on capacity building, workshops, and seminars in the creative and publishing fields. Through this initiative, we aim to empower aspiring writers and foster a culture of creativity and literacy.7. Digital Distribution: Embracing technology, we offer digital distribution services to make our titles accessible to a global audience through online platforms such as Amazon, Takealot.com, Google Books, Barnes & Noble, and Kindle Direct Publishing.8. Publishing Consultations: We provide personalized publishing consultations to guide authors through the publishing process, ensuring that their books meet industry standards and reach their intended audience.9. Timely Delivery: With a project timeline ranging from 35 to 90 days, we are committed to delivering high-quality books within the specified timeframe, thanks to our collaboration with experienced freelancers.At Samnko Books, we are passionate about words and storytelling. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to support authors, promote literacy, and make meaningful contributions to the publishing industry and beyond

How do we work?

How do we work?Here's a breakdown of how the publishing process works at Samnko Books:1. Initial Consultation: The process begins with an initial consultation where we discuss your manuscript, your goals as an author, and the publishing options available to you. This consultation helps us understand your needs and tailor our services accordingly.2. Manuscript Submission: Once you're ready to proceed, you submit your manuscript to us for review. Our team of editors and publishing professionals evaluates your manuscript for quality, marketability, and alignment with our publishing criteria.3. Editing and Formatting: If your manuscript meets our criteria, we move on to the editing and formatting stage. Our experienced editors work closely with you to refine your manuscript, ensuring it meets industry standards in terms of grammar, structure, and style. We also format your manuscript for print and digital distribution. 4. Cover Design: While your manuscript is being edited, our designers work on creating an eye-catching cover for your book. We collaborate with you to ensure the cover design captures the essence of your story and appeals to your target audience.5. Printing and Production: Once the editing, formatting, and cover design are complete, we proceed with the printing and production of your book. We use high-quality materials and state-of-the-art printing technology to ensure your book meets industry standards.6. Distribution: With the printing and production completed, we handle the distribution of your book to various outlets, including schools, libraries, bookstores, and online retailers. Through our partnerships and networks, we ensure your book reaches a wide audience both locally and internationally.7. Digital Distribution: In addition to print distribution, we also offer digital distribution services to make your book available online through platforms such as Amazon, Takealot.com, Google Books, Barnes & Noble, and Kindle Direct Publishing.8. Promotion and Marketing: To support the launch of your book, we provide marketing and promotional services to help you reach your target audience. This may include online advertising, social media promotion, book launch events, and author interviews.9. Royalties and Payments: As your book sells, you receive royalties based on the sales generated. We handle the accounting and payment processes, ensuring you receive your earnings in a timely manner.10. Support and Guidance: Throughout the publishing process, our team is available to provide support and guidance, answering any questions you may have and keeping you informed every step of the way.Overall, our goal at Samnko Books is to provide a seamless and professional publishing experience, helping you bring your book to life and reach your readership effectively
